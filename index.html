Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our services

Who needs to file ITR for FY 2024-25?

Anyone with annual income above ₹2.5 lakh (₹3L for senior citizens, ₹5L for super senior citizens) must file ITR. Also mandatory if you have capital gains, foreign income, or want to claim tax refunds.

What documents do I need for ITR filing?

For salaried: Form 16, Form 26AS, bank statements, investment proofs (80C, 80D). For business: Books of accounts, GST returns, bank statements, expense bills. We'll guide you based on your income type.

How long does ITR filing take?

We file your ITR within 24 hours of receiving all documents. Simple returns can be filed in as little as 3 hours. You'll receive acknowledgment immediately after filing.

What if I receive an income tax notice?

We provide FREE lifetime notice support for all our clients. Our expert CAs will review the notice, prepare the response, and represent you before the Income Tax Department if needed.

Is my data secure on your platform?

Absolutely! We use bank-grade 256-bit SSL encryption, just like TaxBuddy. Your personal and financial data is stored securely and never shared with third parties. We're fully compliant with data protection regulations.

Can I switch between old and new tax regime?

Yes! Our AI automatically calculates tax under both regimes and suggests the one that saves you more money. You can choose while filing. Salaried individuals can switch every year.

Do you help with GST registration and filing?

Yes! We provide complete GST services - new registration, monthly/quarterly returns (GSTR-1, 3B), annual returns (GSTR-9), reconciliation, and compliance management at affordable prices.

What makes you different from other CA services?

We combine AI automation with expert CA review - best of both worlds! Plus 24-hour filing, transparent pricing, free notice support, 365-day assistance, and maximum tax savings. Like TaxBuddy's quality at local CA pricing.
